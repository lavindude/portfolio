About Me!

That year I joined my robotics team, took numerous online courses, and also made my own projects. Currently, I am the Chief Technology Officer of a startup called Veke. I live in Issaquah, Washington. In my freetime, I love running, swimming, hiking, playing chess, and making music with my piano and violin. I also like composing music with Fl Studio.
